Thai Food

Explore the rich and diverse flavors of Thai cuisine. Here are some popular dishes:

  1. Pad Thai: A stir-fried noodle dish commonly served as street food in Thailand.
  2. Tom Yam Koong: A hot and sour Thai soup usually cooked with shrimp.
  3. Keaw Thod (Fried Wonton): Crispy fried wontons often served with a sweet and sour sauce.
  4. Tub Tim Kob Dessert: A refreshing dessert made with water chestnuts in coconut milk.
